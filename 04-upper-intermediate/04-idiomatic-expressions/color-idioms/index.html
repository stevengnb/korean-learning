    Color Idioms¶

    Red (빨강/빨강색/적색)¶

    빨간 맛 - Exciting taste/experience (popularized by Red Velvet song) 

    빨간 맛이 궁금해!
Curious about that red taste! (that exciting feeling)

    빨간 날 - Red-letter day, holiday 

    달력에 빨간 날이 많으면 좋아요.
It's nice when there are many holidays on the calendar.

    빨간 불 - Red light (danger, warning) 

    우리 회사에 빨간 불이 켜졌어요.
Our company is in danger. (red light is on)

    빨간 줄 - Red line (connection, relationship) 

    그들 사이에 빨간 줄이 있대요.
They say there's a red string between them. (destined relationship)

    Blue (파랑/파랑색/청색)¶

    파란만장 - Full of ups and downs, eventful 

    그의 인생은 파란만장했어요.
His life was full of ups and downs.
From: 파란 (blue/green waves) + 만장 (full)

    청춘 - Youth (blue spring) 

    청춘을 즐겨라!
Enjoy your youth!

    청출어람 - Student surpassing teacher (blue from indigo) 

    그 학생은 청출어람이에요.
That student has surpassed the teacher.
From Chinese idiom

    White (하얀/하양/백색)¶

    새하얗다 - Pure white, completely white 

    눈이 와서 산이 새하얘요.
It snowed so the mountain is pure white.

    백지 - Blank slate (white paper) 

    다시 백지에서 시작해요.
Let's start from a blank slate again.

    백발 - White hair (elderly) 

    백발이 되도록 사랑하세요.
Love each other until you have white hair (forever).

    백기를 들다 - Surrender, give up (raise white flag) 

    너무 어려워서 백기를 들었어요.
It was too hard so I gave up.

    백지장도 맞들면 낫다 - Proverb: teamwork helps 

    Even a sheet of paper is lighter when lifted together.

    Black (검정/검정색/흑색)¶

    흑백 - Black and white, clear distinction 

    흑백을 가려야 해요.
We need to distinguish right from wrong.

    흑자 - Surplus, profit (black) 적자 - Deficit, loss (red) 

    올해는 흑자를 냈어요.
This year we made a profit.

Note: Opposite of English red/black accounting

    검은 머리 파뿌리 되도록 - Until death (black hair becomes white roots) 

    검은 머리 파뿌리 되도록 함께해요.
Let's be together until death.

    Yellow/Gold (노랑/금색)¶

    황금 - Gold (valuable) 

    황금 같은 시간
Golden/precious time

황금알을 낳는 거위
The goose that lays golden eggs

    노랗다 - Yellow (also means scared) 

    겁이 나서 얼굴이 노래졌어요.
Their face turned yellow from fear.

    Color Combinations¶

    알록달록 - Colorful, variegated 

    꽃이 알록달록해요.
The flowers are colorful.

    형형색색 - Various colors 

    형형색색의 옷을 입었어요.
Wore clothes of various colors.

    오색찬란 - Five colors brilliant (very colorful) 

    무지개가 오색찬란해요.
The rainbow is brilliantly colorful.

    Gray/Uncertain¶

    회색 지대 - Gray area 

    법의 회색 지대
Gray area of law

    회색빛 - Grayish, uncertain 

    전망이 회색빛이에요.
The outlook is uncertain.

    Cultural Color Meanings¶

    Red¶

    • Celebration, good fortune
    • Passion, excitement
    • Danger, warning

    Blue¶

    • Youth, vitality
    • Sadness (like English "feeling blue")
    • Calm, serenity

    White¶

    • Purity, cleanliness
    • Death, mourning (traditional)
    • New beginning

    Black¶

    • Darkness, evil
    • Elegance, formality
    • Depth, seriousness

    Yellow/Gold¶

    • Wealth, prosperity
    • Autumn, harvest
    • Cowardice (in some contexts)

    Practice Exercises¶

    Exercise 1: Color Meanings¶

    What color is associated with: 1. Holidays on calendar 2. Youth 3. Giving up/surrender 4. Profit in business 5. Eventful life

    Exercise 2: Complete Idioms¶

    1. ___ 기를 들다 (give up)
    2. ___ 만장 (eventful)
    3. ___ 불이 켜지다 (in danger)

    Exercise 3: Use in Context¶

    When would you use these expressions? 1. 흑자를 내다 2. 백지에서 시작하다 3. 빨간 날

    Answer Key

    Exercise 1: 1. Red (빨간 날) 2. Blue (청춘) 3. White (백기를 들다) 4. Black (흑자) 5. Blue (파란만장)

    Exercise 2: 1. 백 2. 파란 3. 빨간

    Exercise 3: 1. When business makes profit 2. When starting over fresh 3. Referring to holidays/days off

    Modern Usage¶

    Some color idioms are evolving: - 빨간 맛 became trendy after K-pop - Yellow associations changing with Western influence - Some traditional meanings fading

    Stay current by noticing how colors are used in contemporary Korean media!
