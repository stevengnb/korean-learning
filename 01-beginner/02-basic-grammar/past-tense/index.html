    Past Tense¶

    Quick Reference

    Speech Level Pattern Example Usage
    Informal Polite Stem + 았/었어요 갔어요, 먹었어요 Daily conversation
    Formal Polite Stem + 았/었습니다 갔습니다, 먹었습니다 Formal situations
    Informal Stem + 았/었어 갔어, 먹었어 Close friends

    Past Tense Formation¶

    Korean past tense is formed by adding 았/었 to the verb stem, following the same vowel harmony rules as present tense.

    Basic Rules¶

    Step 1: Remove 다 from dictionary form Step 2: Apply vowel harmony - Bright vowels (ㅏ, ㅗ) + 았 - Dark vowels (all others) + 었 Step 3: Add 어요 for informal polite or 습니다 for formal polite

    Informal Polite Past Tense (았/었어요)¶

    Regular Verb Conjugation¶

    Dictionary Form Stem Harmony Base Past + 어요 Translation
    가다 가 Bright (ㅏ) 갔 갔어요 went
    오다 오 Bright (ㅗ) 왔 왔어요 came
    보다 보 Bright (ㅗ) 봤 봤어요 saw/watched
    먹다 먹 Dark (ㅓ) 먹었 먹었어요 ate
    서다 서 Dark (ㅓ) 섰 섰어요 stood
    자다 자 Bright (ㅏ) 잤 잤어요 slept
    읽다 읽 Dark (ㅣ) 읽었 읽었어요 read
    쉬다 쉬 Dark (ㅣ) 쉬었 쉬었어요 rested
    배우다 배우 Dark (ㅜ) 배웠 배웠어요 learned
    주다 주 Dark (ㅜ) 줬 줬어요 gave

    Understanding Contractions¶

    When the stem ends in ㅏ or ㅗ, contractions occur:

    Stem + 았 Contracted Form Full Form
    가 + 았 갔 가았 ✗
    자 + 았 잤 자았 ✗
    오 + 았 왔 오았 ✗
    보 + 았 봤 보았 ✗

    하다 Verbs¶

    하다 verbs use a special pattern: 하 + 였 → 했

    Dictionary Form Past Tense Translation
    하다 했어요 did
    공부하다 공부했어요 studied
    일하다 일했어요 worked
    운동하다 운동했어요 exercised
    요리하다 요리했어요 cooked
    청소하다 청소했어요 cleaned
    여행하다 여행했어요 traveled
    전화하다 전화했어요 called

    Formal Polite Past Tense (았/었습니다)¶

    Simply replace 어요 with 습니다.

    Dictionary Form Informal Polite Formal Polite Translation
    가다 갔어요 갔습니다 went
    먹다 먹었어요 먹었습니다 ate
    하다 했어요 했습니다 did
    오다 왔어요 왔습니다 came
    보다 봤어요 봤습니다 saw

    Irregular Verbs in Past Tense¶

    ㄷ Irregular¶

    ㄷ changes to ㄹ before 었.

    Dictionary Form Present Past Translation
    듣다 들어요 들었어요 listened
    걷다 걸어요 걸었어요 walked
    묻다 물어요 물었어요 asked
    싣다 실어요 실었어요 loaded

    Process for 듣다: 1. Stem: 듣 2. ㄷ → ㄹ before vowel: 들 3. Add 었어요: 들었어요

    ㅂ Irregular¶

    ㅂ changes to 우 before 었.

    Dictionary Form Present Past Translation
    춥다 추워요 추웠어요 was cold
    덥다 더워요 더웠어요 was hot
    어렵다 어려워요 어려웠어요 was difficult
    쉽다 쉬워요 쉬웠어요 was easy
    돕다 도와요 도왔어요 helped
    아름답다 아름다워요 아름다웠어요 was beautiful

    Process for 춥다: 1. Stem: 춥 2. ㅂ → 우: 추우 3. Dark vowel → 었: 추웠어요

    ㅡ Irregular¶

    ㅡ drops before 았/었.

    Dictionary Form Present Past Translation
    쓰다 써요 썼어요 wrote/used
    끄다 꺼요 껐어요 turned off
    크다 커요 컸어요 was big
    예쁘다 예뻐요 예뻤어요 was pretty
    슬프다 슬퍼요 슬펐어요 was sad
    기쁘다 기뻐요 기뻤어요 was happy

    Process for 쓰다: 1. Stem: 쓰 2. ㅡ drops: 쓰 → ㅆ 3. Add 었어요: 썼어요

    ㄹ Irregular¶

    ㄹ remains in past tense (only drops before ㄴ, ㅂ, ㅅ in other forms).

    Dictionary Form Present Past Translation
    살다 살아요 살았어요 lived
    만들다 만들어요 만들었어요 made
    알다 알아요 알았어요 knew
    팔다 팔아요 팔았어요 sold
    놀다 놀아요 놀았어요 played

    ㅅ Irregular¶

    ㅅ drops when followed by a vowel.

    Dictionary Form Present Past Translation
    짓다 지어요 지었어요 built
    낫다 나아요 나았어요 got better

    Special Verbs¶

    이다 (to be)¶

    The copula 이다 has unique past tense forms:

    Present Past (Informal Polite) Past (Formal Polite)
    학생이에요 학생이었어요 학생이었습니다
    의사예요 의사였어요 의사였습니다

    Note: After a vowel, 이에요 → 예요, and 이었어요 → 였어요

    있다/없다 (to exist/to not exist)¶

    Verb Present Past Translation
    있다 있어요 있었어요 was/existed
    없다 없어요 없었어요 wasn't/didn't exist

    Examples with Context¶

    Completed Actions¶

    1. 어제 친구를 만났어요. (I met a friend yesterday.)
    2. 지난주에 영화를 봤어요. (I watched a movie last week.)
    3. 아침에 빵을 먹었어요. (I ate bread this morning.)
    4. 작년에 한국에 갔어요. (I went to Korea last year.)
    5. 어젯밤에 일찍 잤어요. (I slept early last night.)
    6. 주말에 집을 청소했어요. (I cleaned the house on the weekend.)

    Past States and Descriptions¶

    1. 어제 날씨가 추웠어요. (The weather was cold yesterday.)
    2. 시험이 어려웠어요. (The exam was difficult.)
    3. 그 사람이 친절했어요. (That person was kind.)
    4. 음식이 맛있었어요. (The food was delicious.)

    Narrative (What I Did Today)¶

    1. 오늘 아침 7시에 일어났어요. (I woke up at 7 AM this morning.)
    2. 샤워를 했어요. (I took a shower.)
    3. 커피를 마셨어요. (I drank coffee.)
    4. 8시에 학교에 갔어요. (I went to school at 8.)
    5. 수업을 들었어요. (I attended class.)
    6. 점심에 친구들을 만났어요. (I met friends at lunch.)
    7. 도서관에서 공부했어요. (I studied in the library.)
    8. 저녁에 집에 왔어요. (I came home in the evening.)
    9. 저녁을 먹었어요. (I ate dinner.)
    10. TV를 봤어요. (I watched TV.)

    Question Forms¶

    1. 어제 뭐 했어요? (What did you do yesterday?)
    2. 주말에 어디 갔어요? (Where did you go on the weekend?)
    3. 점심 먹었어요? (Did you eat lunch?)
    4. 숙제 다 했어요? (Did you finish your homework?)

    Practice¶

    1. Conjugate to past tense (informal polite): 가다, 먹다, 자다, 읽다
    2. Conjugate to past tense: 듣다, 걷다, 묻다
    3. Conjugate to past tense: 춥다, 돕다, 어렵다
    4. Conjugate to past tense: 쓰다, 크다, 예쁘다
    5. Translate to Korean: "I went to school yesterday."
    6. Translate to Korean: "The weather was hot last week."
    7. Create a sentence using: 공부하다 (past tense)
    8. Explain the conjugation process: 듣다 → 들었어요
    9. Convert to formal polite: 갔어요, 먹었어요, 했어요
    10. Fill in the past tense: 저는 어제 친구를 _____. (만나다)
    Show Answers 1. Past tense conjugations: - 가다 → 갔어요 (가 + 았 → 갔) - 먹다 → 먹었어요 - 자다 → 잤어요 (자 + 았 → 잤) - 읽다 → 읽었어요 2. Past tense (ㄷ irregular): - 듣다 → 들었어요 (ㄷ → ㄹ before 었) - 걷다 → 걸었어요 (ㄷ → ㄹ before 었) - 묻다 → 물었어요 (ㄷ → ㄹ before 었) 3. Past tense (ㅂ irregular): - 춥다 → 추웠어요 (ㅂ → 우, then + 었) - 돕다 → 도왔어요 (ㅂ → 우 + ㅏ → 와, then + 았) - 어렵다 → 어려웠어요 (ㅂ → 우, then + 었) 4. Past tense (ㅡ irregular): - 쓰다 → 썼어요 (ㅡ drops, then + 었) - 크다 → 컸어요 (ㅡ drops, then + 었) - 예쁘다 → 예뻤어요 (ㅡ drops, then + 었) 5. 저는 어제 학교에 갔어요. 6. 지난주에 날씨가 더웠어요. 7. Sample answers: - 어제 한국어를 공부했어요. (I studied Korean yesterday.) - 도서관에서 공부했어요. (I studied in the library.) - 3시간 동안 공부했어요. (I studied for 3 hours.) 8. 듣다 → 들었어요 conjugation process: - Step 1: Remove 다 → 듣 - Step 2: ㄷ irregular - ㄷ changes to ㄹ before vowel → 들 - Step 3: Dark vowel (ㅡ) → use 었 - Step 4: Add 어요 → 들었어요 9. Formal polite conversions: - 갔어요 → 갔습니다 - 먹었어요 → 먹었습니다 - 했어요 → 했습니다 10. 저는 어제 친구를 만났어요. - 만나다 (stem: 만나, bright vowel ㅏ) - 만나 + 았 → 만났 - 만났 + 어요 → 만났어요
