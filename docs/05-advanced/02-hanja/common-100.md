## Practice

### Recognizing Compound Words

Can you guess the meaning?

1. 학교 (學校) = study + school = ?
2. 생일 (生日) = birth + day = ?
3. 동물 (動物) = move + thing = ?
4. 한국인 (韓國人) = Korea + country + person = ?
5. 출입구 (出入口) = exit + enter + mouth = ?

**Answers:** 1. school, 2. birthday, 3. animal, 4. Korean person, 5. entrance/exit

## Study Tips

1. **Learn radicals first**: Many hanja share components
2. **Group by theme**: Numbers, time, people, etc.
3. **Focus on recognition**: Don't worry about writing
4. **See patterns**: Same hanja appears in many words
5. **Use flashcards**: Spaced repetition works well

Knowing these 100 characters unlocks **thousands of Korean words**!
