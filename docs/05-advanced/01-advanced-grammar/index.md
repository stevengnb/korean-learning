# Advanced Grammar

Advanced grammar patterns appear in formal writing, literature, legal documents, and classical Korean. While not used in everyday conversation, understanding these patterns is essential for reading sophisticated Korean texts.

## What You'll Learn

**Literary Forms** - Patterns from literature and classical texts
**Archaic Endings** - Historical forms still appearing in modern Korean
**Formal Particles** - Specialized particles for formal contexts
**Complex Patterns** - Sophisticated grammatical constructions

## Study Focus

These patterns are primarily for:
- **Recognition** (reading comprehension)
- **Formal writing** (academic, legal, literary)
- **Cultural understanding** (classical texts, historical documents)

Most are not for everyday conversation.

## Sections

- Literary forms and expressions
- Archaic sentence endings
- Formal particles (께서, (으)로서, (으)로써)
