# Specialized Korean

Korean for professional and specialized contexts requires domain-specific vocabulary and conventions. This section covers the most common specialized areas.

## Areas Covered

### Business Korean
- Corporate communication
- Meetings and presentations
- Negotiations
- Business correspondence
- Office vocabulary

### Academic Korean
- Research and writing
- Academic presentations
- Thesis writing
- Citation and references
- Scholarly discussion

### Media Korean
- News language
- Broadcasting
- Journalism
- Interview techniques
- Media terminology

## Approach

Each specialized area has:
- **Unique vocabulary** specific to the field
- **Conventional expressions** used in that context
- **Formality expectations** appropriate to the setting
- **Cultural norms** for professional interaction

## Study Focus

Choose based on your needs:
- **Business professionals** → Business Korean
- **Students/researchers** → Academic Korean
- **Media industry** → Media Korean

Master the vocabulary and conventions relevant to your field for professional-level Korean competency.
