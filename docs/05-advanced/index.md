# Advanced Korean

Welcome to advanced Korean. At this level, you'll explore sophisticated grammar, learn hanja (Chinese characters) essential for deeper understanding, and master specialized vocabulary for professional and academic contexts.

## What Makes This "Advanced"?

Advanced Korean involves:
- **Literary and archaic forms** rarely used in daily speech
- **Hanja (한자)** understanding for academic/formal Korean
- **Specialized vocabulary** for professional fields
- **Cultural and historical depth** in language use
- **Native-level nuance** and sophistication

## Sections

### Advanced Grammar
Complex patterns found in:
- Literature and formal writing
- Legal and academic texts
- Classical Korean
- Formal speeches
- Archaic forms still appearing in modern Korean

### Hanja (Chinese Characters)
Understanding 한자 helps with:
- Academic vocabulary (70% of Korean words have Chinese roots)
- Newspaper reading
- Formal documents
- Professional terminology
- Etymology and word relationships

### Specialized Korean
Professional language for:
- Business and commerce
- Academic research
- Media and journalism
- Technical fields

## Study Approach

**At this level:**
1. Focus on your specific needs (business, academic, etc.)
2. Read extensively in your field
3. Build specialized vocabulary systematically
4. Understand hanja for word relationships
5. Practice formal/sophisticated expression

## Moving Forward

Choose sections based on your goals:
- **Academic path**: Focus on academic style + hanja
- **Business path**: Business Korean + formal grammar
- **General advancement**: Broad exposure to all sections

Let's reach native-level proficiency!
