# Addition Conjunctions

!!! abstract "Quick Reference"
    | Pattern | Meaning | Formality |
    |---------|---------|-----------|
    | -ㄹ 뿐만 아니라 | not only... but also | Formal |
    | -(으)ㄹ 뿐더러 | not only... but also | Formal/Written |
    | -는 데다가 | in addition to, on top of | Neutral |
    | -(으)며 | and (simultaneous/listing) | Formal |
    | -고도 | and yet, despite doing | Neutral |

These patterns add information, emphasizing that multiple things are true or happening.

## -ㄹ 뿐만 아니라

"Not only... but also" — The most common addition pattern.

### Formation

| Verb Type | Formation | Example |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Action verb | Stem + -ㄹ 뿐만 아니라 | 먹을 뿐만 아니라 |
| Descriptive verb | Stem + -ㄹ 뿐만 아니라 | 예쁠 뿐만 아니라 |
| Noun + 이다 | Noun + 일 뿐만 아니라 | 학생일 뿐만 아니라 |
| Past | -았/었을 뿐만 아니라 | 갔을 뿐만 아니라 |

### Examples

| Korean | English |
|--------|---------|
| 그는 똑똒할 뿐만 아니라 성실하다 | He is not only smart but also diligent |
| 이 식당은 맛있을 뿐만 아니라 싸다 | This restaurant is not only delicious but also cheap |
| 한국어를 배울 뿐만 아니라 일본어도 배운다 | I learn not only Korean but also Japanese |
| 그녀는 가수일 뿐만 아니라 배우이기도 하다 | She is not only a singer but also an actress |

### Shortened Form: -ㄹ 뿐 아니라

In speech, 만 is often dropped:
- 예쁠 뿐 아니라 착하다 (not only pretty but also kind)

---

## -(으)ㄹ 뿐더러

Same meaning as -ㄹ 뿐만 아니라, but more formal/literary.

### Formation

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Vowel stem | 가 + ㄹ 뿐더러 → 갈 뿐더러 |
| Consonant stem | 먹 + 을 뿐더러 → 먹을 뿐더러 |
| ㄹ stem | 살 + 뿐더러 → 살 뿐더러 |

### Examples

| Korean | English |
|--------|---------|
| 비가 올 뿐더러 바람도 분다 | Not only is it raining, but it's also windy |
| 그 사람은 부자일 뿐더러 인기도 많다 | That person is not only rich but also popular |
| 피곤할 뿐더러 배도 고프다 | I'm not only tired but also hungry |

---

## -는 데다가

"In addition to, on top of" — Adds cumulative information, often building toward a conclusion.

### Formation

| Type | Formation | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Action (present) | -는 데다가 | 먹는 데다가 |
| Action (past) | -(으)ㄴ 데다가 | 먹은 데다가 |
| Descriptive | -(으)ㄴ 데다가 | 예쁜 데다가 |
| Noun | 인 데다가 | 학생인 데다가 |

### Examples

| Korean | English |
|--------|---------|
| 비가 오는 데다가 춥기까지 하다 | On top of it raining, it's even cold |
| 늦은 데다가 준비도 안 했다 | On top of being late, I wasn't prepared either |
| 맛있는 데다가 양도 많다 | It's delicious and on top of that, the portions are big |
| 월요일인 데다가 아침이라 사람이 많다 | Since it's Monday and on top of that morning, there are many people |

### Nuance
-는 데다가 often implies the combined factors lead to a result or conclusion.

---

## -(으)며

"And" — Formal connector for listing or simultaneous actions.

### Formation

| Stem ending | Formation | Example |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Vowel | -며 | 가며 |
| Consonant | -으며 | 먹으며 |

### Two Uses

**1. Listing (and)**
| Korean | English |
|--------|---------|
| 그는 의사이며 교수이다 | He is a doctor and (also) a professor |
| 서울은 크며 복잡하다 | Seoul is large and complex |

**2. Simultaneous actions**
| Korean | English |
|--------|---------|
| 음악을 들으며 공부한다 | I study while listening to music |
| 웃으며 말했다 | (He) said while smiling |

### Comparison: -며 vs -고

| -며 | -고 |
|-----|-----|
| More formal | More casual |
| Written/news | Spoken |
| Emphasizes simultaneity | Sequential or simple listing |

---

## -고도

"And yet, despite doing" — Adds information with a nuance of contrast or unexpectedness.

### Formation

Verb stem + -고도

### Examples

| Korean | English |
|--------|---------|
| 알고도 모른 척했다 | Despite knowing, (he) pretended not to know |
| 먹고도 배가 고프다 | Even after eating, I'm still hungry |
| 듣고도 못 들은 척했다 | Despite hearing, (she) pretended not to hear |
| 보고도 믿을 수 없다 | Even seeing it, I can't believe it |

### Nuance
-고도 implies "even though X happened, Y is still the case" — often unexpected.

---

## Comparison Chart

| Pattern | Nuance | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| -ㄹ 뿐만 아니라 | A and also B (neutral addition) | 예쁠 뿐만 아니라 착하다 |
| -는 데다가 | A, and on top of that B (cumulative) | 비가 오는 데다가 춥다 |
| -(으)며 | A and B (formal listing) | 크며 아름답다 |
| -고도 | Despite A, still B (unexpected) | 알고도 모른 척했다 |

## Summary

| When to use | Pattern |
|-------------|---------|
| "Not only A but also B" | -ㄹ 뿐만 아니라 |
| "A, and on top of that B" | -는 데다가 |
| Formal listing/simultaneous | -(으)며 |
| "Despite A, still B" | -고도 |
