# Causative Verbs (사동사)

!!! abstract "Quick Reference"
    **Causative suffixes**: -이-, -히-, -리-, -기-, -우-, -추-

    **Meaning**: Make/let/have someone do something

    - 먹다 → 먹이다 (to feed)
    - 입다 → 입히다 (to dress someone)
    - 자다 → 재우다 (to put to sleep)
    - 앉다 → 앉히다 (to seat someone)

## Detailed Explanation

### What is Causative Voice?

Causative voice indicates that the subject causes or allows someone else to perform an action. Instead of doing something directly, you make or let someone else do it.

**Structure:**
```
Subject + Object + 에게/한테 + Causative Verb
엄마가 아기에게 밥을 먹여요.
(Mom feeds the baby / makes the baby eat.)
```

### Key Characteristics

1. **Indirect action**: The subject doesn't perform the main action directly
2. **Object marker**: Usually uses 에게, 한테 for the person being caused to act
3. **Dual interpretation**: Can mean "make" (force) or "let" (allow)

## -이- Causative Verbs

| Active Verb | Causative Verb | Meaning |
|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 먹다 | 먹이다 | to feed (make eat) |
| 보다 | 보이다 | to show (make see) |
| 쌓다 | 쌓이다 | to pile up (make stack) |
| 쓰다 (write) | 쓰이다 | to make write |
| 씻다 | 씻기다 | to wash (someone) |
| 신다 | 신기다 | to put shoes on (someone) |
| 벗다 | 벗기다 | to take off (someone's clothes) |

**Examples:**

1. 엄마가 아기에게 밥을 먹여요.
   (Mom feeds the baby.)

2. 선생님이 학생들에게 사진을 보여 주세요.
   (Teacher, please show the students the photo.)

3. 아이에게 옷을 입혀야 해요.
   (I need to dress the child.)

4. 할머니가 손자에게 신발을 신겨 주셨어요.
   (Grandma put shoes on her grandchild.)

5. 아기를 씻겨야 돼요.
   (I have to wash the baby.)

## -히- Causative Verbs

| Active Verb | Causative Verb | Meaning |
|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 입다 | 입히다 | to dress (someone) |
| 읽다 | 읽히다 | to make read |
| 앉다 | 앉히다 | to seat (someone) |
| 맡다 | 맡기다 | to entrust |
| 맞다 | 맞히다 | to make correct/guess |
| 놓다 | 놓이다 | to place, let go |
| 숨다 | 숨기다 | to hide (something) |

**Examples:**

1. 아이에게 따뜻한 옷을 입혔어요.
   (I dressed the child in warm clothes.)

2. 선생님이 학생들에게 책을 읽혔어요.
   (The teacher had the students read the book.)

3. 손님을 의자에 앉혔어요.
   (I seated the guest in a chair.)

4. 아이를 할머니한테 맡겼어요.
   (I entrusted the child to grandma.)

5. 정답을 맞혔어요.
   (I got the answer right / I guessed correctly.)

6. 진실을 숨기면 안 돼요.
   (You shouldn't hide the truth.)

## -리- Causative Verbs

| Active Verb | Causative Verb | Meaning |
|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 울다 | 울리다 | to make cry, to ring |
| 알다 | 알리다 | to inform, notify |
| 날다 | 날리다 | to fly (something) |
| 살다 | 살리다 | to save (a life) |
| 돌다 | 돌리다 | to turn (something) |
| 물다 | 물리다 | to make bite |
| 밀다 | 밀리다 | to push |
| 걸다 | 걸리다 | to hang (something) |

**Examples:**

1. 아기를 울렸어요.
   (I made the baby cry.)

2. 종을 울렸어요.
   (I rang the bell.)

3. 친구들에게 소식을 알렸어요.
   (I informed my friends of the news.)

4. 연을 날렸어요.
   (I flew a kite.)

5. 의사가 환자를 살렸어요.
   (The doctor saved the patient.)

6. 손잡이를 돌렸어요.
   (I turned the handle.)

## -기- Causative Verbs

| Active Verb | Causative Verb | Meaning |
|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 안다 | 안기다 | to hold (someone) |
| 웃다 | 웃기다 | to make laugh |
| 옮다 | 옮기다 | to move (something) |
| 맡다 | 맡기다 | to entrust |
| 감다 | 감기다 | to wrap |
| 담다 | 담기다 | to put in, fill |

**Examples:**

1. 아기를 안겼어요.
   (I held the baby / let someone hold the baby.)

2. 친구가 저를 웃겼어요.
   (My friend made me laugh.)

3. 책상을 옮겼어요.
   (I moved the desk.)

4. 짐을 택배로 맡겼어요.
   (I entrusted the luggage to delivery service.)

## -우- Causative Verbs

This suffix is used primarily with certain verbs where other suffixes don't apply.

| Active Verb | Causative Verb | Meaning |
|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 자다 | 재우다 | to put to sleep |
| 서다 | 세우다 | to stand (something) up, to stop |
| 타다 (burn) | 태우다 | to burn (something) |
| 타다 (ride) | 태우다 | to give a ride |
| 깨다 | 깨우다 | to wake (someone) up |
| 맞다 | 맞추다 | to adjust, match |
| 춥다 | 춥히다 | to make cold |
| 맵다 | 맵히다 | to make spicy |

**Examples:**

1. 아기를 재웠어요.
   (I put the baby to sleep.)

2. 차를 세웠어요.
   (I stopped the car.)

3. 쓰레기를 태웠어요.
   (I burned the trash.)

4. 친구를 차에 태웠어요.
   (I gave my friend a ride in my car.)

5. 동생을 깨웠어요.
   (I woke up my younger sibling.)

6. 시계를 맞췄어요.
   (I set/adjusted the clock.)

## -추- Causative Verbs

Less common suffix, mainly with adjectives.

| Active Verb | Causative Verb | Meaning |
|-------------|----------------|---------|
| 낮다 | 낮추다 | to lower |
| 높다 | 높이다 | to raise, heighten |
| 넓다 | 넓히다 | to widen |
| 줄다 | 줄이다 | to reduce |
| 늘다 | 늘이다 | to increase |

**Examples:**

1. 목소리를 낮췄어요.
   (I lowered my voice.)

2. 가격을 높였어요.
   (They raised the price.)

3. 도로를 넓혔어요.
   (They widened the road.)

4. 지출을 줄였어요.
   (I reduced spending.)

5. 근무 시간을 늘렸어요.
   (They increased working hours.)

## Usage Context

### Make vs. Let

Context determines whether causative means "make" (force) or "let" (allow):

**Make (force):**
- 엄마가 저를 공부하게 했어요. (Mom made me study.)
- 아이를 재웠어요. (I put the child to sleep - gently forced)

**Let (allow):**
- 아이를 놀게 했어요. (I let the child play.)
- 친구에게 책을 보여 줬어요. (I showed my friend the book - allowed to see)

### Common Contexts

**1. Childcare:**
- 먹이다 (feed), 씻기다 (wash), 입히다 (dress), 재우다 (put to sleep)

**2. Service/Business:**
- 맡기다 (entrust), 태우다 (give a ride), 알리다 (notify)

**3. Teaching:**
- 읽히다 (make read), 쓰게 하다 (make write), 배우게 하다 (make learn)

**4. Adjustments:**
- 낮추다 (lower), 높이다 (raise), 줄이다 (reduce), 늘이다 (increase)
