# Result

!!! abstract "Quick Reference"
    | Pattern | Meaning | Formation | Example |
    |---------|---------|-----------|---------|
    | **-게 되다** | Come to, end up | Stem + 게 되다 | 알게 되다 (come to know) |
    | | Gradual change/result | | 한국어를 잘하게 됐어요 |
    | | (often unintentional) | | |
    | **-아/어지다** | Become (change) | 아/어 + 지다 | 좋아지다 (become better) |
    | | State/quality change | | 날씨가 추워졌어요 |
    | | (natural change) | | |

    **Key Difference**: -게 되다 = gradual result/process; -아/어지다 = direct change in state

## Detailed Explanation

Both patterns express changes and results, but they focus on different aspects of the change process.

### -게 되다 (Come To, End Up)

**Formation**: Verb/Adjective stem + 게 되다

- 알다 → 알게 되다 (come to know)
- 먹다 → 먹게 되다 (come to eat)
- 좋다 → 좋게 되다 (become good)

**Key Features:**

- **Process emphasized**: Shows how something came about
- **Gradual change**: Change happened over time
- **External factors**: Often implies circumstances led to the result
- **Less direct control**: Not entirely by choice
- **Can be intentional or unintentional**

**Common Uses:**

1. Learning/acquiring abilities
2. Circumstantial changes
3. Life transitions
4. Realizations and understanding

**Tense Conjugations:**

- Present: -게 돼요 (will come to)
- Past: -게 됐어요 (came to)
- Future: -게 될 거예요 (will come to)

### -아/어지다 (Become, Get)

**Formation**: Attach 아/어 (based on vowel harmony) + 지다

- 좋다 → 좋아지다 (become good/better)
- 나쁘다 → 나빠지다 (become bad/worse)
- 크다 → 커지다 (become big/bigger)
- 작다 → 작아지다 (become small/smaller)

**Key Features:**

- **State change focus**: Emphasizes the new state
- **Natural progression**: Change happens naturally
- **Observable change**: Can see or feel the difference
- **Mostly adjectives**: Primarily used with adjectives
- **Some verbs allowed**: 잊다 → 잊어지다 (be forgotten)

**Common Pattern:**

More/less + adjective-아/어지다

- 더 좋아지다 (get better)
- 덜 추워지다 (get less cold)

**Tense Conjugations:**

- Present: -아/어져요 (becomes)
- Past: -아/어졌어요 (became)
- Future: -아/어질 거예요 (will become)

### Formation Steps

**For -게 되다:**

1. Take the verb/adjective stem
2. Add -게
3. Add conjugated form of 되다
4. Conjugate 되다 for tense

**For -아/어지다:**

1. Take the adjective stem (or specific verbs)
2. Apply vowel harmony: 아 or 어
3. Add -지다
4. Conjugate -지다 for tense

### Examples

**-게 되다 (Gradual Result/Process):**

- 한국어를 잘하게 됐어요 (I came to speak Korean well)
- 그 사람을 알게 됐어요 (I came to know that person)
- 한국 음식을 좋아하게 됐어요 (I came to like Korean food)
- 매일 운동하게 됐어요 (I ended up exercising every day)
- 진실을 알게 될 거예요 (You'll come to know the truth)
- 그 영화를 보게 됐어요 (I ended up watching that movie)
- 이해하게 됐어요 (I came to understand)
- 일찍 일어나게 됐어요 (I came to wake up early)

**-아/어지다 (Change in State):**

- 날씨가 추워졌어요 (The weather became cold)
- 한국어가 쉬워졌어요 (Korean became easier)
- 길이 복잡해졌어요 (The road became complicated)
- 유명해졌어요 (became famous)
- 건강해졌어요 (became healthy)
- 조용해졌어요 (became quiet)
- 예뻐졌어요 (became pretty)
- 많아졌어요 (became more/many)

**Comparing Both:**

Similar situations, different nuances:

- 한국어를 잘하게 됐어요 (I came to be good at Korean - through experience/time)
- 한국어가 잘해졌어요 (My Korean got better - observable improvement)

**With Time Expressions:**

- 시간이 지나면서 익숙해졌어요 (It became familiar as time passed)
- 3년 동안 한국어를 잘하게 됐어요 (Over 3 years, I came to speak Korean well)
- 요즘 날씨가 따뜻해졌어요 (The weather has gotten warm lately)

**Complex Examples:**

- 한국에 살게 되면서 김치를 좋아하게 됐어요 (While living in Korea, I came to like kimchi)
- 공부하다 보니 쉬워지게 됐어요 (As I studied, it came to be easier)
- 연습을 많이 해서 실력이 좋아졌어요 (Because I practiced a lot, my skills got better)
- 나이가 들면서 건강의 중요성을 알게 됐어요 (As I got older, I came to know the importance of health)

### Special Cases

**Passive-like with -아/어지다:**

- 문이 열어졌어요 (The door opened/became opened)
- 문제가 해결됐어요 (The problem was solved)
- 이름이 잊어졌어요 (The name was forgotten)

**Can't always interchange:**

❌ 키가 크게 됐어요 (Wrong)
✅ 키가 커졌어요 (I grew taller)

❌ 알아지다 (Doesn't exist)
✅ 알게 되다 (come to know)

### Common Expressions

**With -게 되다:**

- 만나게 되다 (come to meet)
- 생각하게 되다 (come to think)
- 느끼게 되다 (come to feel)
- 깨닫게 되다 (come to realize)

**With -아/어지다:**

- 좋아지다 (get better)
- 나빠지다 (get worse)
- 많아지다 (increase)
- 적어지다 (decrease)
- 길어지다 (get longer)
- 짧아지다 (get shorter)

## Practice

1. (알다) 한국 문화를 _____ 됐어요.

2. (춥다) 날씨가 _____어요.

3. (좋아하다) 김치를 _____ 됐어요.

4. (쉽다) 한국어가 _____어요.

5. Translate: "I came to understand Korean grammar."

6. (많다) 학생들이 _____어요.

7. (생각하다) 건강이 중요하다고 _____ 됐어요.

8. (조용하다) 방이 _____어요.

9. Translate: "The problem became difficult."

10. (만나다) 좋은 친구들을 _____ 됐어요.

<summary>Show Answers</summary>

1. 알게 - 한국 문화를 **알게** 됐어요.
   - I came to know Korean culture (gradual learning process)

2. 추워졌 - 날씨가 **추워졌**어요.
   - The weather became cold (observable state change)

3. 좋아하게 - 김치를 **좋아하게** 됐어요.
   - I came to like kimchi (gradual change in preference)

4. 쉬워졌 - 한국어가 **쉬워졌**어요.
   - Korean became easier (state change)

5. 한국어 문법을 이해하게 됐어요.
   - 이해하다 → 이해하게 되다 (gradual understanding)

6. 많아졌 - 학생들이 **많아졌**어요.
   - The students became many/increased (quantity change)

7. 생각하게 - 건강이 중요하다고 **생각하게** 됐어요.
   - I came to think that health is important (realization over time)

8. 조용해졌 - 방이 **조용해졌**어요.
   - The room became quiet (state change)

9. 문제가 어려워졌어요.
   - 어렵다 → 어려워지다 (direct state change)

10. 만나게 - 좋은 친구들을 **만나게** 됐어요.
    - I came to meet good friends (circumstantial result)
