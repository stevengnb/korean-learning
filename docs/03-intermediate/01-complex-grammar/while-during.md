# While/During

!!! abstract "Quick Reference"
    | Pattern | Meaning | Formation | Example |
    |---------|---------|-----------|---------|
    | **-면서** | While (simultaneous) | Stem + 면서 | 걸으면서 (while walking) |
    | | Same subject only | | 음악을 들으면서 공부해요 |
    | **-는 동안** | During, while | Stem + 는 동안 | 자는 동안 (while sleeping) |
    | | Different subjects OK | | 제가 자는 동안 눈이 왔어요 |
    | **-다가** | While then (switch) | Stem + 다가 | 먹다가 (while eating, then) |
    | | Interruption/change | | 공부하다가 잤어요 |

    **Key Difference**: -면서 = doing both together; -는 동안 = time frame; -다가 = switch actions

## Detailed Explanation

These patterns express temporal relationships between actions, but each has distinct uses and nuances.

### -면서 (While, Simultaneous Actions)

**Formation**: Verb stem + 면서

- 가다 → 가면서
- 먹다 → 먹으면서
- 공부하다 → 공부하면서

**Key Features:**

- **Same subject**: The same person does both actions
- **Simultaneous**: Both actions happen at the same time
- **Both actions are deliberate**: Not accidental overlap

**Usage:**

Cannot be used with:
- Adjectives (only action verbs)
- Different subjects doing different actions
- Sequential actions (use -고 instead)

### -는 동안 (During, While)

**Formation**:
- Action verbs: Stem + 는 동안
- Adjectives: Stem + (으)ㄴ 동안
- Past: Stem + (으)ㄴ/았던/었던 동안

**Examples:**
- 가다 → 가는 동안
- 크다 → 큰 동안
- 먹었다 → 먹은 동안

**Key Features:**

- **Different subjects allowed**: Each clause can have its own subject
- **Time frame focus**: Emphasizes the duration
- **Works with adjectives**: Unlike -면서
- **More formal** than -면서

### -다가 (While Then, Switching Actions)

**Formation**: Verb stem + 다가

- 가다 → 가다가
- 먹다 → 먹다가
- 공부하다 → 공부하다가

**Key Features:**

- **Interruption**: First action interrupted or stopped
- **Sequential change**: Switch from one action to another
- **Unexpected events**: Often used with surprises or accidents
- **Same subject typically**

**Common Uses:**

1. Interrupted actions
2. Changed plans
3. Unexpected discoveries
4. Accidents during an action

### Formation Steps

**For -면서:**

1. Take the verb stem
2. Add -면서
3. Add second clause (same subject)

**For -는 동안:**

1. Identify if verb or adjective
2. Add -는 동안 (verbs) or -(으)ㄴ 동안 (adjectives)
3. Add second clause (any subject)

**For -다가:**

1. Take the verb stem
2. Add -다가
3. Add second clause showing change/interruption

### Examples

**-면서 (Simultaneous Actions):**

- 음악을 들으면서 공부해요 (I study while listening to music)
- 걸으면서 이야기해요 (We talk while walking)
- 커피를 마시면서 책을 읽었어요 (I read a book while drinking coffee)
- 웃으면서 말했어요 (He spoke while smiling)
- 운전하면서 전화하지 마세요 (Don't use the phone while driving)

**-는 동안 (During a Period):**

- 제가 자는 동안 눈이 왔어요 (It snowed while I was sleeping)
- 방학인 동안 여행할 거예요 (I'll travel during vacation)
- 엄마가 요리하는 동안 청소했어요 (I cleaned while mom was cooking)
- 어렸을 동안 서울에 살았어요 (I lived in Seoul while I was young)
- 회의하는 동안 조용히 하세요 (Be quiet during the meeting)

**-다가 (Switching/Interruption):**

- 공부하다가 잤어요 (I fell asleep while studying)
- 걷다가 친구를 만났어요 (I met a friend while walking)
- 영화를 보다가 울었어요 (I cried while watching the movie)
- 밥을 먹다가 전화를 받았어요 (I answered the phone while eating)
- 한국에 살다가 미국으로 이사했어요 (I moved to America while living in Korea)

**Comparing All Three:**

Same situation, different nuances:

- 음악을 들으면서 공부해요 (I study while listening to music - both intentional)
- 음악을 듣는 동안 공부했어요 (I studied during the time I listened to music - time frame)
- 음악을 듣다가 공부했어요 (I was listening to music, then switched to studying)

**Complex Examples:**

- 한국에 사는 동안 한국어를 배웠어요 (I learned Korean during my time living in Korea)
- 걸으면서 생각했어요 (I thought while walking)
- 책을 읽다가 좋은 구절을 발견했어요 (I discovered a good passage while reading)
- 일하는 동안 음악을 들으면서 해요 (During work, I do it while listening to music)

### Common Mistakes

❌ 공부하면서 잤어요 (Can't be simultaneous - sleeping interrupts studying)
✅ 공부하다가 잤어요

❌ 엄마가 요리하면서 저는 청소했어요 (Different subjects)
✅ 엄마가 요리하는 동안 저는 청소했어요

❌ 예쁜면서 똑똑해요 (Can't use -면서 with adjectives for "and")
✅ 예쁘고 똑똑해요

## Practice

1. (듣다, simultaneous) 음악을 _____ 달리기를 해요.

2. (자다, during) 제가 _____ 친구가 왔어요.

3. (걷다, then) 길을 _____ 넘어졌어요.

4. Translate: "I watch TV while eating." (simultaneous)

5. (공부하다, during) _____ 조용히 해 주세요.

6. (읽다, then) 책을 _____ 잠이 들었어요.

7. Translate: "I met my teacher while shopping."

8. (일하다, simultaneous) 노래를 부르_____ 일해요.

9. (비가 오다, during) _____ 집에 있었어요.

10. (보다, then) 영화를 _____ 웃었어요.

<summary>Show Answers</summary>

1. 들으면서 - 음악을 **들으면서** 달리기를 해요.
   - I run while listening to music (simultaneous, same subject)

2. 자는 동안 - 제가 **자는 동안** 친구가 왔어요.
   - My friend came while I was sleeping (different subjects, time frame)

3. 걷다가 - 길을 **걷다가** 넘어졌어요.
   - I fell while walking (interruption/accident during action)

4. 먹으면서 텔레비전을 봐요. / 텔레비전을 보면서 먹어요.
   - Both actions done simultaneously by same person

5. 공부하는 동안 - **공부하는 동안** 조용히 해 주세요.
   - Please be quiet while (I'm) studying (time frame)

6. 읽다가 - 책을 **읽다가** 잠이 들었어요.
   - I fell asleep while reading (unintentional interruption)

7. 쇼핑하다가 선생님을 만났어요. / 쇼핑하는 동안 선생님을 만났어요.
   - -다가 emphasizes the unexpected meeting; -는 동안 is also acceptable

8. 부르면서 - 노래를 **부르면서** 일해요.
   - I work while singing (both actions simultaneous and intentional)

9. 비가 오는 동안 - **비가 오는 동안** 집에 있었어요.
   - I stayed home during the time it was raining

10. 보다가 - 영화를 **보다가** 웃었어요.
    - I laughed while watching the movie (spontaneous reaction during movie)
