## Basic Food Terms

### Staple Foods

| Korean | Meaning | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| 밥 | Rice / Meal | Also means "meal" in general |
| 국 | Soup | Clear soup |
| 찌개 | Stew | Thicker than 국 |
| 김치 | Kimchi | Fermented vegetables |
| 빵 | Bread | From English "bread" |
| 면 | Noodles | General term |
| 밥 | Cooked rice | Staple food |

**Examples:**
- 밥 먹었어요? (Did you eat? - common greeting)
- 국이 뜨거워요. (The soup is hot.)
- 김치가 매워요. (The kimchi is spicy.)

## Tastes and Descriptions

### Basic Taste Adjectives

| Korean | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 맛있다 | Delicious | 맛있어요 |
| 맛없다 | Not delicious | 맛없어요 |
| 달다 | Sweet | 달아요 |
| 짜다 | Salty | 짜요 |
| 맵다 | Spicy | 매워요 |
| 시다 | Sour | 셔요 |
| 쓰다 | Bitter | 써요 |
| 싱겁다 | Bland | 싱거워요 |

**Examples:**
- 이 음식은 맛있어요. (This food is delicious.)
- 너무 매워요. (It's too spicy.)
- 좀 짜요. (It's a bit salty.)
- 안 매워요? (Isn't it spicy?)

### Temperature and Texture

| Korean | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 뜨겁다 | Hot (temperature) |
| 차갑다 | Cold |
| 따뜻하다 | Warm |
| 시원하다 | Cool, refreshing |
| 부드럽다 | Soft, tender |
| 바삭하다 | Crispy |
| 질기다 | Chewy, tough |

**Examples:**
- 국이 뜨거워요. (The soup is hot.)
- 시원한 물 주세요. (Please give me cold/refreshing water.)
- 고기가 부드러워요. (The meat is tender.)

## Restaurant Vocabulary

### Ordering

| Korean | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 주문하다 | To order |
| 메뉴 | Menu |
| 주문할게요 | I'll order |
| 이거 주세요 | Please give me this |
| ...개 주세요 | Please give me X items |
| ...인분 | Serving for X people |

**Examples:**
- 메뉴판 좀 주세요. (Please give me the menu.)
- 주문할게요. (I'll order now.)
- 비빔밥 2인분 주세요. (Two servings of bibimbap, please.)
- 이거 하나 주세요. (One of this, please.)

### During the Meal

| Korean | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 잘 먹겠습니다 | I will eat well (before eating) |

| 맛있게 드세요 | Enjoy your meal (polite) |
| 물 좀 주세요 | Please give me water |
| 더 주세요 | Please give me more |

| Korean | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 계산하다 | To pay / calculate bill |
| 계산해 주세요 | Check, please |
| 얼마예요? | How much is it? |

**Examples:**
- 계산해 주세요. (Check, please.)

- 포장해 주세요. (Please pack it to go.)

## Common Phrases

### Expressing Preferences

- 좋아해요 (I like it)
- 싫어해요 (I dislike it)
- 못 먹어요 (I can't eat it)
- 안 먹어요 (I don't eat it)
- 배불러요 (I'm full)
- 배고파요 (I'm hungry)
- 목말라요 (I'm thirsty)

**Examples:**
- 매운 음식을 좋아해요. (I like spicy food.)
- 고기를 못 먹어요. (I can't eat meat.)
- 배불러요. 더 못 먹어요. (I'm full. I can't eat more.)

### Dietary Restrictions

- 채식주의자 (vegetarian)
- 비건 (vegan)
- 알레르기 (allergy)
- 고기 안 먹어요 (I don't eat meat)
- 해산물 알레르기가 있어요 (I have a seafood allergy)
