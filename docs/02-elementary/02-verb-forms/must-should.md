# Must and Should Expressions

!!! abstract "Quick Reference"
    - **-아/어야 하다/되다**: must, have to, should (obligation/necessity)
    - **-면 안 되다**: must not, cannot (prohibition)
    - **-아/어야 하다** is stronger obligation than **-아/어야 되다**
    - Attach to verb stems, follow vowel harmony rules

## Detailed Explanation

### -아/어야 하다/되다 (Must/Have to/Should)

These patterns express obligation, necessity, or strong recommendation.

**Formation:**
- Verb stem + -아/어야 + 하다/되다
- Follow standard vowel harmony (ㅏ/ㅗ → 아야, all others → 어야)
- 하다 verbs → 해야

**하다 vs 되다:**
- **-아/어야 하다**: Stronger obligation, external requirement
- **-아/어야 되다**: Slightly softer, more conversational

**Conjugation:**
```
가다 → 가야 하다/되다 (must go)
먹다 → 먹어야 하다/되다 (must eat)
공부하다 → 공부해야 하다/되다 (must study)
```

### -면 안 되다 (Must Not/Cannot)

Expresses prohibition or strong warning against an action.

**Formation:**
- Verb stem + -면 안 되다
- Literally: "if you do X, it won't do/work"

**Examples:**
```
가다 → 가면 안 되다 (must not go)
먹다 → 먹으면 안 되다 (must not eat)
말하다 → 말하면 안 되다 (must not speak)
```

### When to Use

**-아/어야 하다/되다:**
- Giving advice or recommendations
- Expressing personal obligations
- Stating rules or requirements
- Describing necessary conditions

**-면 안 되다:**
- Stating prohibitions or rules
- Warning against dangerous actions
- Expressing things that are not allowed
- Setting boundaries

### Examples

**Obligations:**
- 내일 학교에 가야 해요. (I have to go to school tomorrow.)
- 숙제를 해야 돼요. (I need to do my homework.)
- 약을 먹어야 해요. (You should take medicine.)
- 한국어를 열심히 공부해야 해요. (You must study Korean diligently.)

**Prohibitions:**
- 여기서 담배를 피우면 안 돼요. (You must not smoke here.)
- 시험 중에 말하면 안 돼요. (You cannot talk during the exam.)
- 이 물을 마시면 안 돼요. (You must not drink this water.)
- 늦게 자면 안 돼요. (You shouldn't go to bed late.)

**Dialogue:**
```
A: 내일 약속이 있어요?
B: 네, 병원에 가야 해요.
A: 아파요?
B: 감기 때문에 의사를 만나야 해요.

A: Do you have plans tomorrow?
B: Yes, I have to go to the hospital.
A: Are you sick?
B: I need to see a doctor because of a cold.
```

## Practice

1. Complete: 저는 매일 운동을 ______. (I have to exercise every day.)

2. Complete: 도서관에서 ______ 안 돼요. (You must not be loud in the library.) [시끄럽다]

3. Translate: You must wake up early tomorrow.

4. Complete: 건강하려면 야채를 많이 ______. (To be healthy, you must eat lots of vegetables.) [먹다]

5. Complete: 수업 시간에 전화를 ______ 안 돼요. (You cannot use your phone during class.) [사용하다]

6. Translate: I need to meet my friend at 3 o'clock.

7. Complete: 비가 오니까 우산을 ______. (Since it's raining, you should bring an umbrella.) [가지고 가다]

8. Complete: 시험 중에 ______ 안 돼요. (You must not cheat during the exam.) [컨닝하다]

9. Create a sentence using -아/어야 해요 about something you need to do this week.

10. Create a sentence using -면 안 돼요 about a rule in your home or workplace.
