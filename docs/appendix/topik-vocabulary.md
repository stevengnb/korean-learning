## Study Strategy by TOPIK Level

### TOPIK I (Beginner)
- Focus on **500-1000 most common words**
- Daily life vocabulary
- Basic verbs and adjectives
- Numbers, time, days

### TOPIK II Level 3-4 (Intermediate)
- Expand to **2000-3000 words**
- Abstract concepts
- Connecting words
- Topic-specific vocabulary

### TOPIK II Level 5-6 (Advanced)
- Master **5000+ words**
- Specialized vocabulary
- Sino-Korean words
- Formal expressions
- Idiomatic expressions

## Learning Tips

1. **Use spaced repetition** - Review regularly
2. **Learn in context** - Not just translations
3. **Group by topic** - Easier to remember
4. **Practice writing** - Reinforce memory
5. **Read authentic materials** - See words in use
6. **Make word families** - 공부, 공부하다, 공부방

## Essential TOPIK Phrases

### For Writing
- 첫째/둘째/셋째 (first/second/third)
- 예를 들어 (for example)
- 따라서/그러므로 (therefore)
- 결론적으로 (in conclusion)
- ~에 따르면 (according to)

### For Reading Comprehension
- ~을/를 통해 (through)
- ~에 대해 (about, regarding)
- ~으로 인해 (due to)
- ~에도 불구하고 (despite)
- ~는 반면에 (whereas)

Building strong vocabulary is key to TOPIK success!
