## Study Tips

**Daily Practice:**
1. Anki review (20 min)
2. Grammar lesson (30 min)
3. Listening practice (20 min)
4. Reading (20 min)
5. Writing/speaking practice (20 min)

**Immersion:**
- Change phone/computer to Korean
- Watch K-dramas with Korean subs
- Listen to Korean music daily
- Follow Korean social media

**Active Learning:**
- Use a language journal
- Practice speaking (even alone!)
- Write short texts daily
- Find a language partner

## Recommended Learning Path

**Beginner (0-6 months):**
- TTMIK Levels 1-3 OR Integrated Korean 1
- Anki for vocabulary
- Simple K-drama with subs
- Basic grammar focus

**Intermediate (6-18 months):**
- TTMIK Levels 4-7 OR Integrated Korean 2-3
- Korean Grammar in Use (Intermediate)
- K-dramas with Korean subs
- Start reading webtoons
- Language exchange practice

**Advanced (18+ months):**
- Advanced textbooks
- Korean Grammar in Use (Advanced)
- News, novels, academic texts
- TOPIK II preparation
- Regular conversation practice

## Free vs Paid

**Best Free Resources:**
- TTMIK (lessons 1-10)
- How to Study Korean
- Anki
- YouTube channels
- Library textbooks

**Worth Paying For:**
- TTMIK workbooks
- Professional tutor (italki)
- Grammar in Use series
- Quality textbooks
- TOPIK practice materials

The key to success is **consistency** - daily practice, even 20 minutes, beats sporadic intensive study!
